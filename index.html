My name is Khin Poe Poe Tun and nickname is Poe. Currently, I'm studying interior architecture at school of architecture and design(KMUTT). Before I came to apply in KMUTT, I was studying civil engineering for 2.5 years and went to construction site for a year. Then, I started to learn interior design in myanmar for 6 months and worked at In-house furniture store. After that, I got Presidential international scholarship in KMUTT and currently studying there. While I studying in KMUTT, I got th third prize in Dhammamonkol temple competition group project.
